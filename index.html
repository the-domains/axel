welcome to the machine

spontaneous filth from outside the bubble

They will never find me. They will never find me.

I have no one else to talk to. So I talk to you, page. La Page. Say it with a French accent.

Sounds like la plage, the beach. You are my beach. My bitch.

No one is going to find me here. So it all ends up, forgotten, hiding. Reluctant. We are born with such fire and we lose it all day long. Every day, except the good ones. Until we find the bottom. I had such hope, until I bought my first screen. Now I think like a chip. On and Off. YES NO answers only please. Por Favor. Do me a favor. Say it with a French accent. Faveur.

Why are there two worlds? The one we see and the one we SEE. The things we want or the things we crave. Real or Imagined? as if it was a question. Such a false dichotomy. Nothing is real. Everything is imagined. Next question.

I am just tired of the lying and the hatred. Ever since we stabbed Caesar with the rich men’s knives we have been lying about what we do and why we do it. I don’t even think it’s most people that always want more - it’s just a few people. But the idea is contagious and once the blood is in the water only a storm can set the matter to rest. Money. Stuff. Trash. Reality Television. You can throw money at anything and it gets bigger, more grotesque. Bulging at the belly and always hungry for more of the things we crave. And always covered in lies.

Until the lights go out.

And on this day.

I found a snippet of poetry from a few days ago:

if I ever go

don't say a word

turn off the light

and close the door

don't shed a tear

for what came before

I gave so much but

I could have given more

I think I’m working it out. Just starting to get there. Glad I didn’t give up.

Years later I think, and I am back. Some dates would be nice. Time stamps and such. Do I want to upgrade to v3 for life? For 90 something dollars?

So here we are. No save?
